About

We are a Visual Design Collaborative based in Ghent, where we proudly graduated as bachelors in Visual Design from the esteemed LUCA School of Arts. We describe ourselves as a dynamic duo whose skillsets are well balanced and compatible. With intent and typographic excellence, we explore the boundaries and meaning of design and art.


Our approach is bold. We challenge conventions while embracing nuance—bringing sharp precision and unexpected playfulness together. Every project is a deliberate balance of strength and subtlety, crafted to make a lasting impression.

Multi-facetted in our approach, we adapt seamlessly to every client. From daring experimental visuals to thoughtful branding or refined corporate identities, every project is shaped with care and creativity to meet your needs.


Design is a solution — a means to reach a target. We create work that connects, endures, and sparks emotion and intellect with every interaction.
